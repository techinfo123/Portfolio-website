About Me

I'm Atishay Jain, a passionate technologist crafting the future with code. As a GenAI Engineer and Robotics Lead, I specialize in building systems that can think, reason, and learn — from LLM-powered agents to autonomous robots that navigate the real world.

Currently pursuing my B.Tech in ECE at JIIT, I’ve interned at Orbo.AI and AIensured.com, where I led initiatives in OCR, ML pipelines, and model interpretability. My stack includes TensorFlow, Streamlit, LangChain, MLflow and I’m actively working with tools like CrewAI, FastAPI, AutoGen, and Hugging Face.

As the Technical Lead at uCR Robotics Hub, I lead a team in designing and deploying real-world autonomous and manual robotic solutions showcased in college expos. I thrive on innovation and mentorship — pushing limits, breaking silos, and transforming abstract ideas into AI-driven products.

Beyond the code, I believe in ethical AI, system reliability, and storytelling through interactive, immersive interfaces. You're not just seeing my work — you're experiencing my vision. Let’s connect and build the future.
